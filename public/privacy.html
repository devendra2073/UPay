UPay Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 13, 2026

1. Introduction

At UPay, we prioritize the trust and security of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our payment gateway aggregator services designed for the Indian market.

2. Information We Collect

  • Transaction Data: Details of payments made through our platform, including amounts, timestamps, and merchant details.
  • Payment Information: We process UPI IDs (VPA), intent data, and integration details with third-party providers like Paytm.
  • Security Data: Data processed through secure authentication methods, including Google reCAPTCHA and Google Sign-In.
  • Technical Details: IP addresses and device information to ensure secure authentication and fraud prevention.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to provide and improve our financial services:

  • Facilitating seamless UPI intent generation and transaction routing.
  • Verifying identities via secure login protocols.
  • Complying with Indian financial regulations and digital payment guidelines.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. Information is only shared with trusted partners like banks and integrated payment providers (e.g., Paytm) to complete transactions, or when required by Indian law.

5. Data Security

Your data is protected using industry-standard encryption. We host our services on secure environments to ensure that your payment information remains confidential and safe from unauthorized access.

Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this policy or your data rights, please reach out to the UPay support team.
